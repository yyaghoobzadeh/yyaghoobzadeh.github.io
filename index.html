Since November 2017, I am a Researcher at Microsoft Research Montreal.
I got my PhD from CIS, LMU Munich under supervision of Prof. Hinrich Schütze, working mainly on the problem of fine-grained entity typing.
Before PhD, I did my M.Sc at Sharif University of Technology and my B.Sc at University of Tehran, both in Tehran, Iran.
My main research interests are representation learning, text-based knowledge base completion, distributional semantics, deep learning and its applications in natural language understanding.
