I'm currently an assistant professor at ECE, University of Tehran. Before, I was a senior researcher at Microsoft Montreal, where I worked on natural language processing (NLP) and deep learning, particularly on word, sentence and sentence pair semantics.

Before joining Microsoft in 2017, I did my PhD in computer science at CIS, University of Munich (LMU) and my MSc at Sharif University of Technology and BSc at University of Tehran.

As professional activities, I co-organized two workshops on Subword and Character level models in NLP (SCLeM) at EMNLP 2017 and NAACL 2018. I was Area Chair at ACL 2021 and Remote Presentation Co-chair at the same venue. I have also served as a program committee member at various NLP and AI conferences: ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, AAAI, CIKM, COLING, EACL, CoNLL, LREC.
